Environment
Why Go Electric?
Modern climate concerns require changes in the traditional vehicle industry. Emissions from fossil fuels have plagued the planet, trapping it in a blanket of greenhouse gases that are warming the climate at historically high rates. One of the most practical and effective methods of reducing fossil fuel emissions is choosing an electric vehicle. As more people become environmentally conscious and switch to electric, demand for oil and gasoline lessens and a transition away from fossil fuel use is made increasingly possible.
Environmetally Friendly
The carbon emissions from traditional gas vehicles are perpetuating the climate problem. Switching to electric power nullifies this emission and protects the environment to ensure a healthy, functional planet for future generations.
Efficiency
EVs are significantly more efficient in power transmission, creating near instant torque that cannot be matched by traditional gasoline vehicles. Additionally, electric motors are much lighter than gasoline engines and require much less power to initially accelerate, making EVs much more efficient for the average consumer.
Performance
The instant torque, smooth handling, and fast acceleration of EVs offer a driving experience that cannot be replicated by traditional gasoline vehicles. In addition to increased road performance, EVs are both easier and cheaper to repair, considering the relative efficiency and simplicity of an electric motor compared to a combustion engine.
VIS is more than the average car manufacturer. We specialize in electric vehicles built tough, economical, and most importantly, green. Our goal is to provide quality vehicles that transcend our competition in value, performance, and environmental consciousness.

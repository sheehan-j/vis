Performance
Our SUVs feature all-wheel drive, delivering powerful acceleration and speed that traditional gasoline vehicles fail to match. VIS SUVs are designed for the long haul, featuring a driving range of 355 miles. Our high capacity batteries keep consumers on the road longer, so you can avoid pesky repeated stops at charging stations. The impressive design and manufacturing quality of our vehicles often outcompetes major manufacturers in terms of performance.
Safety
Our SUV models have adapted many of the safety features of our sedan models, including traffic monitoring, automatic lane centering, blind spot cameras, and adaptive cruise control. We have added a Pedestrian Crash Avoidance Mitigation (PCAM) system to our SUVs that will give consumers increased visibility and monitoring of surroundings.
